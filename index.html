Research

I am interested in hardware/software methodology and architecture design for security and reliability of embedded processors, including:
  • Trustworthy RISC-V processor and System-on-Chip architecture
  • Temporal and spatial memory safety
  • Processor architecture for soft errors

Short Bio

I obtained my PhD in computer science and engineering from the University of New South Wales in Australia in 2014. Before that, I was a postgraduate student at Shanghai Jiaotong University in China. I graduated at Hefei University of Technology in China with B.E. in electronic science and technology in 2008.
