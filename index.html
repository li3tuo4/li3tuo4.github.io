Research

I am interested in hardware/software methodology and architecture design for security and reliability of embedded processors. In particular:
  • Trustworthy RISC-V processor and System-on-Chip architecture
  • Temporal and spatial memory errors
  • Soft errors
  • Design automation for security and reliability
