About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the State Key Laboratory of Computer Architecture, at the Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), CAS. I received a PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of New South Wales (UNSW) in 2014, under the supervision of Prof. Sri Parameswaran. I am very grateful to have the opportunity to work with CES at KIT led by Prof. Jörg Henkel during my PhD study. Before joining ICT CAS, I was a Research Associate at UNSW, where I worked as the Tech Lead/Architect on two trustworthy computer architecture projects, with DSTG Australia. I am a contributor of the RISC-V Rocket Chip. I review for conferences and journals, such as CCS, DAC, ICCAD, DATE, TC, TCAD, TVLSI, etc. My publication records can be found at DBLP and Google Scholar. My open-source activites can be found at GitHub.

Research

My main research areas are computer architecture, computer security, fault tolerance. My recent works are regarding security-enhanced RISC-V processor architectures against timing side channels and memory safety vulnerabilities.
